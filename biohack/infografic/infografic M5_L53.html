📈 Cortizol diurn — normal vs. stres cronic vs. burnout

Normal: CAR amplificat 08:00; scadere progresiva pana la miezul noptii. Ritm circadian intact.
Stres cronic: Baseline ridicat tot timpul. CAR amplificat patologic. Ritm partial pastrat dar supra-activat.
Burnout: CAR blunt (atenuat). Baseline scazut. Axa HPA epuizata — nu mai raspunde. DHEA-S scazut.
